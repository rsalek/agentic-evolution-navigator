# Agent payments are moving from protocol to production

Open standards and institutional backing are being joined by early live transactions. The thesis remains medium-confidence because evidence of repeated usage, merchant breadth, dispute handling, and economic value is still limited.

## Supporting evidence

- [[wiki/events/2026-07-02 BBVA and Visa complete agent-initiated payment]] is direct production evidence.
- [[wiki/events/2026-07-17 x402 Foundation expands institutional backing]] broadens the protocol ecosystem.

## Challenging evidence

- No seed event yet demonstrates scaled, repeated agent-payment activity with independently verified economics.

## What would change the thesis

- Raise confidence after repeat transaction, active-agent, merchant, and exception-rate data.
- Lower confidence if production trials stall or delegated authority proves operationally unacceptable.
