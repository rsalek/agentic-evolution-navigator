# Talkspace measures AI behavioral triage outcomes

Talkspace reported a test in which its AI model improved identification and response to high-risk behaviors by 50%, increased therapeutic quality by 47%, and produced 3x higher user satisfaction versus the base model. The workflow retained real-time risk determination, live therapist referral, clinical oversight, and HIPAA protection.

## Why it matters

This is quantitative pilot evidence with an explicit safety and escalation process. It is a test result, not proof of scaled clinical impact.
