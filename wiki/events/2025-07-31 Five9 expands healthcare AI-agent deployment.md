# Five9 expands healthcare AI-agent deployment

Five9 described a leading academic health system expanding after three years of success, with Five9 and Epic integrated to streamline clinical workflows. The initial order was expected to produce more than $1.1 million in ARR.

## Why it matters

This is academic-health-system production expansion with system-of-record integration and an explicit commercial signal. The customer is not named in the capture, and the disclosure does not isolate the share of ARR attributable to AI agents.

## Evidence

- [Five9 Q2 2025 earnings call](https://web.quartr.com/companies/3631/events/341105/overview) - primary-source transcript capture.
- [Five9 launches new integration with Epic Toolbox](https://www.five9.com/news/news-releases/five9-launches-new-integration-epic-toolbox) - first-party release corroborating the concrete Epic workflow integration; it does not independently verify the unnamed customer's ARR or scaled agent outcomes.
