# ServiceNow agentifies customer production workflows

ServiceNow described forward-deployed engineers agentifying workflows inside customer production environments. The cited examples were NTT Data keeping 70,000 configuration items under compliance, PayPal processing trillions of payments faster, and Robinhood supporting onboarding during acquisition integration and 26% headcount growth.

## Why it matters

The disclosure connects agent deployment to a system-of-record platform and named operating environments. The figures describe the surrounding customer operations, not necessarily agent-attributed outcome lift.
