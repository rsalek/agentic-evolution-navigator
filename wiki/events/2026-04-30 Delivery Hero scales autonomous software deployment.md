# Delivery Hero scales autonomous software deployment

Delivery Hero reported that its internal Herogen agent can take a feature description from an engineer or product owner through the full software-development lifecycle and deploy it end to end in the company's app. Management put its annual coding output at the equivalent of 130 engineers and said output was growing at a double-digit weekly rate.

## Why it matters

This is operator-side evidence of an agent causing production software state changes outside customer-service operations. It therefore challenges the current concentration thesis, while one company disclosure does not establish a broad market shift.

The engineer-equivalent measure is not accompanied by accepted-change volume, defect, rollback, security, review-effort, or direct financial-impact data. It may also capture surrounding platform and process improvements rather than the agent alone.
