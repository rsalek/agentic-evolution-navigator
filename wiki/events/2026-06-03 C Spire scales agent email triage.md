# C Spire scales agent email triage

ServiceNow reported that C Spire automated 70% to 72% of inbound email-case triage and routing through three sequential agents. Triage time fell 44%, from 54 minutes to 30 minutes; first-line case closure increased 125%; and more than 283 staff hours per month were returned.

The third agent surfaces possible duplicates and major incidents for human verification rather than closing every case autonomously.

## Why it matters

The case supplies named scaled deployment evidence, recurring automation share, operating-time improvement, first-line outcomes, and an explicit human boundary inside an incumbent workflow platform.

The evidence is a vendor-published customer story. Its measurement window, denominator, error rate, and independent customer confirmation are not public.
