# Singtel scales Shirley across support and roaming transactions

Singtel reported that its Sierra-powered Shirley agent handled more than 70,000 cases in its first six weeks. It resolved 73% of mobile and home troubleshooting cases without an officer, completed 76% of roaming sign-ups without an officer, and completed more than 200 roaming add-on purchases.

## Why it matters

This is customer-side evidence of repeated service execution and transaction-adjacent state changes, not only question answering. The deployment went live in under ten weeks and retained customer verification, security controls, and human oversight.

The release describes outbound voice sales as a future expansion, so that capability is not treated as live adoption evidence.
