# BBVA and Visa complete agent-initiated payment

BBVA reported completing its first AI-agent-initiated transaction together with Visa. This is evidence of a live transaction, but not yet scaled recurring activity.

## Why it matters

The event moves agentic payments beyond membership and protocol discussion into a production transaction involving a bank and a payment network.
