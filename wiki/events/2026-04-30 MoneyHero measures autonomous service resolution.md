# MoneyHero measures autonomous service resolution

MoneyHero reported that AI automation touched up to 70% of customer-service queries and successfully resolved 47% without human intervention in December 2025.

## Why it matters

This is operator-side evidence with a defined monthly period and an autonomous-resolution denominator. It strengthens the case that bounded customer-service workflows are a leading area of scaled agent use.

The same filing reported 12% year-over-year growth in approved applications and a 32% decline in employee-benefit expense, but it attributed those outcomes to business mix, restructuring, operating leverage, and AI adoption together. The graph therefore does not treat either financial change as agent-caused.

## Evidence

- [MoneyHero Group reports fourth quarter and full year 2025 results](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1974044/000121390026049716/ea028713101ex99-1.htm) - primary operator SEC filing.
