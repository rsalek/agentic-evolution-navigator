# DataDome reports 45 percent Q2 agent-traffic growth

DataDome reported that AI-agent traffic increased 45 percent in the second quarter and associated the increase with adoption of agent-trust policies.

## Why it matters

This is a rare quantitative traffic signal, but it is issuer-reported. The methodology, customer mix, traffic classification, and denominator must be checked before it is treated as a market-wide index.
