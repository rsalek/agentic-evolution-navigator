# Production agent migration improves cost and speed

Ploy published a production-agent migration case study reporting 2.2 times faster execution and 27 percent lower cost.

## Why it matters

This is a unit-economics signal rather than a capability announcement. It should remain medium-confidence until workload definitions, baseline, reliability, and total supervision cost are independently examined.

## Evidence

- [Migrating a production AI agent to GPT-5.6: 2.2x faster, 27% cheaper](https://ploy.ai/blog/migrating-a-production-ai-agent-to-gpt-5-6) - primary case study, discussed on Hacker News.
