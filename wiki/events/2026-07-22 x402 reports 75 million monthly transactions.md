# x402 reports 75 million monthly transactions

The x402 Foundation homepage displayed 75.41 million transactions and USD 24.24 million in volume for the preceding 30 days when accessed on 22 July 2026.

## Why it matters

The counters are direct quantitative evidence that the protocol is processing repeated economic activity at scale, moving the x402 chain beyond institutional-backing announcements.

The page does not publish methodology, historical snapshots, independent verification, active payer or merchant counts, exception rates, or the share initiated by AI agents rather than people or conventional applications. The figures therefore establish protocol activity, not verified agent-payment volume.
