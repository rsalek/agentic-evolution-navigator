# Five9 scales healthcare AI-agent commitment

Five9 said a healthcare provider increased its commitment from about $6 million to more than $10 million in ARR under a three-year commitment, while using AI agents to drive cost savings across the business.

## Why it matters

This is unusually clear economic evidence for a scaled agent deployment, though the company did not disclose the agent-attributed portion of the ARR or savings.
