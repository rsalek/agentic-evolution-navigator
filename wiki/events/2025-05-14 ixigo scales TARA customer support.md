# ixigo scales TARA customer support

Le Travenues Technology reported that TARA resolved more than 5 million user queries during FY 2024-25 and 90.5% of support queries. It also reported autonomous resolution of more than 60% of voice interactions and nearly 90% of chats.

## Why it matters

The annual report supplies direct operator evidence of high-volume multilingual support automation across voice and chat.

The same report labels TARA chat and voice as beta in another section and does not reconcile the denominators or define “resolved.” TARA's lineage as a chatbot also creates rebranding risk, so the graph classifies only the demonstrated support function. Future agentic trip management and booking claims are excluded.
