# Lufthansa operates scaled service-agent network

NICE Cognigy reports that Lufthansa Group operates more than 16 customer-service AI agents handling more than 16 million conversations a year, with peaks of 375,000 conversations a day. The multilingual, multi-channel workflows include travel information, alternative-flight searches, rebooking, and refunds.

## Why it matters

The case shows a multi-agent service network operating at substantial, recurring volume and executing state-changing travel workflows rather than only answering questions.

The usage evidence is vendor-authored and does not separate completed resolutions or transactions from conversations. NICE management also described direct savings and avoided manual hours on its Q1 call, but no published calculation was located, so those economic figures are not promoted.
