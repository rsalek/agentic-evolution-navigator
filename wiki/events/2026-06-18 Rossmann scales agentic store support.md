# Rossmann scales agentic store support

ServiceNow reported that Rossmann had six agents live in store support. The system automatically categorised and prioritised 89% of incoming tickets across more than 200 service categories, reached 98% routing accuracy, and reduced labour cost by 50% on AI-handled cases.

ServiceNow also cited a projected annual saving of EUR 20 million to EUR 30 million. That forecast is kept separate from the realised routing and labour-cost claims.

## Why it matters

The case connects repeated agent use to frontline retail operations, structured service categories, autonomous closure for some issues, and human handling for exceptions.

The source is a vendor-published customer story. The cost methodology, measurement period, realised annual savings, autonomous completion rate, and independent customer confirmation remain undisclosed.
