SuccessKPI announced workforce engagement management for Webex Contact Center, combining planning, quality management, coaching, analytics, and conversational intelligence for hybrid human and AI operations.

## Why it matters

The event illustrates an enterprise operating model in which AI agents are coordinated and evaluated alongside human workers, rather than deployed as a separate channel.
