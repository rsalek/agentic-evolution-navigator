# NatWest reports higher autonomous Cora resolution

NatWest reported that its GenAI-enhanced Cora journey resolved around 20 percentage points more customer queries without human intervention than the equivalent non-GenAI-supported Cora journey during the 2025 annual-report period.

## Why it matters

The comparison supplies operator-side production evidence and a named baseline rather than a vendor capability claim. It supports the service-operations pattern but does not establish scale because absolute query volume is undisclosed.

Journey redesign, routing, or changes in query mix could explain part of the improvement. The report also omits corrections, later recovery, customer outcomes, control settings, and economic effects.
