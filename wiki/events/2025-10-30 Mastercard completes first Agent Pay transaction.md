# Mastercard completes first Agent Pay transaction

Mastercard reported that Agent Pay completed its first agentic transaction on the network during the quarter. It said U.S. Bank and Citibank cardholders could use Agent Pay and named Walmart as a partner.

## Why it matters

This is direct network-level production evidence with named issuers and a merchant partner. It establishes a live transaction, not scaled agent-payment volume.
