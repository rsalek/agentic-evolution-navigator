# Talkspace deploys clinically supervised AI intake workflow

Talkspace described an AI Agent Intake workflow with risk assessment, consent, safety and quality controls, escalation, and live therapist handoff. The company said outputs are validated by licensed clinicians within its QMS and that the workflow is HIPAA compliant.

## Why it matters

This is a concrete human-in-the-loop deployment pattern for a consequential healthcare workflow. It demonstrates controlled autonomy rather than unsupervised clinical decision-making.
