# Falabella scales automated customer-service resolution

Falabella reported more than 115,000 interactions in a regional GenAI-enabled automated-chat workflow that had been live since October. It said 65% of those interactions were resolved without human intervention.

## Why it matters

This is direct operator reporting with a task-volume denominator, operating period, and autonomous-resolution rate. It strengthens the pattern of scaled adoption in bounded service workflows and reduces reliance on vendor-authored customer stories.

The report does not define regional scope, complexity mix, correction or later-recovery rates, controls, or economic effects. The architecture may also include conventional chatbot or deterministic automation, so the graph records the operational outcome rather than inferring broad agent autonomy.
