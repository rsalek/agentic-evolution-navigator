# Agentic Marketing Automation

Marketing work executed by agents across customer data, campaign workflows, channels, and measurement systems.

## Observable signals

- Campaigns or journeys executed with bounded human intervention.
- Conversion lift, time-to-launch, content-review rate, and cost per outcome.
- Native access to shared customer records and enterprise workflows.
