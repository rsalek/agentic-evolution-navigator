# Agentic Evolution Navigator

This context describes how evidence about AI-agent adoption and economic activity is represented so that developments can be connected and interpreted over time.

## Language

**Event**:
A dated, verifiable occurrence that can change the understood state of the agent economy.
_Avoid_: Update, news item, story

**Claim**:
A source-attributed statement whose truth may be supported, challenged, or superseded.
_Avoid_: Fact, takeaway

**Entity**:
An identifiable actor such as an organization, product, protocol, platform, or regulator.
_Avoid_: Company when the actor is not a company

**Concept**:
A reusable mechanism, use case, market layer, monetization model, or constraint that can appear across events.
_Avoid_: Topic, category

**Thesis**:
A revisable interpretation of several events and claims that expresses a larger market dynamic.
_Avoid_: Fact, conclusion, theme

**Theme**:
A stable area of inquiry that organizes related concepts without asserting that they are true.
_Avoid_: Thesis

**Evidence**:
A traceable source record used to establish an event or evaluate a claim.
_Avoid_: Content, link

**Evidence Contract**:
A routing-only assessment of which workflow, operational, impact, and control dimensions a passage appears to contain, which dimensions are absent, and what verification route should follow.
_Avoid_: Proof, adoption score, automatic promotion

**Workflow Anatomy**:
The observable chain from a named trigger through agent actions and affected systems to a resulting state change, including human and control boundaries.
_Avoid_: Use case when execution details are unknown

**Operational Proof**:
Primary-source evidence that a named workflow is live or completed, ideally with task volume, outcome, reliability, control, or economic measurements.
_Avoid_: Product availability, intention, vendor capability

**Evidence Gap**:
A material missing dimension—such as denominator, time window, control boundary, correction rate, or attribution—that limits maturity or confidence.
_Avoid_: Absence of evidence as negative evidence

**Admission Route**:
The next review state assigned by the evidence contract, such as discovery only, announcement watchlist, operational watchlist, primary-verification candidate, or rebranding rejection.
_Avoid_: Acceptance decision

**Source Role**:
The epistemic role a source plays: discovery lead, primary operator evidence, primary vendor evidence, regulator or standards evidence, or independent corroboration.
_Avoid_: Treating all primary-looking documents as equivalent

**Maturity Stage**:
The observed degree of operational adoption: announcement, pilot, production, or scaled.
_Avoid_: Progress, status

**Update Chain**:
An ordered sequence of events that describe how the same initiative or market development changes over time.
_Avoid_: Timeline when the events are not causally related

**Signal**:
A measured observation used as a proxy for adoption, usage, economic value, or friction.
_Avoid_: Proof

**Graph Projection**:
A derived representation of canonical wiki nodes and typed relations for navigation or computation.
_Avoid_: Source of truth, database
